Do you sleep with that thing on?

When I walk around New York City wearing Google Glass, I get a lot different reactions to it. I wanted to start tracking these reactions and what better way to do it than using Google's Mirror API? Anytime someone says something to me about Google Glass, I can tap into my application and dicatate their quote, which will automatically list here.

If you'd like to contribute, please click here. After approving this Glassware with your account, a new card will appear in your timeline. You can pin that card so that it's easily accessible, just to the left of the "ok glass" card.
